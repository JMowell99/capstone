Problem Statement


Design, build, and test a device to track vitals to ensure a person is keeping healthy and alerting them if not. We need to be able to have constant contact with the person's skin, high accuracy, long battery life for everyday use, and enough area to integrate the system on. The device will be composed of discrete components and chips that are integrated to form a whole system which will be powered by a FlexEnergy battery and energy harvester in a hybrid fashion.
