Heart Rate

Heart rate refers to the number of times the heart beats per minute, and it indicates the strength and efficiency of the heart's pumping function. The normal range for heart rate in adults is 60 to 100 beats per minute, with variations depending on age, physical activity, and health conditions.

Oxygen Level

Oxygen level, measured through pulse oximetry as SpO2, reflects the percentage of oxygen saturation in the blood. A healthy range is 95% to 100%. Deviations may signal respiratory or circulatory issues, making regular monitoring important.

Step Count

Step count quantifies daily physical activity by tallying steps taken. It's a simple indicator of movement and exercise, often tracked by fitness devices. Monitoring step count supports fitness goals, weight management, and overall well-being, promoting an active lifestyle.
