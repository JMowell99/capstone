Heart Rate

Heart rate refers to the number of times the heart beats per minute, and it indicates the strength and efficiency of the heart's pumping function. The normal range for heart rate in adults is 60 to 100 beats per minute, with variations depending on age, physical activity, and health conditions.

Respiration Rate

Respiration rate refers to the number of breaths an individual takes per minute. It measures how well the body is able to exchange oxygen and carbon dioxide. The normal range for respiration rate in adults is 12 to 20 breaths per minute, with variations based on age, physical activity, and health conditions.
